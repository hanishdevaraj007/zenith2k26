ZENITH 2K26 is a national-level technical symposium that brings together the brightest minds from across the country. With a diverse range of technical and non-technical events, ZENITH 2K26 offers participants an opportunity to showcase their skills, compete at the highest level, and network with industry professionals and fellow enthusiasts. Join us as we push the boundaries of what's possible.

Registration Guidelines

Read before you register. Every rule matters.

  1. To participate in ZENITH 2K26, you must be a currently enrolled student at any recognized institution in India.
  2. Team events require a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 5 members per team. Only one registration per team is needed.
  3. Participants can register for multiple events, but must adhere to the event schedule and avoid overlapping registrations.
  4. All registrations are final and non-refundable. However, team compositions can be modified up to 24 hours before the event.
  5. Participants must bring a valid ID proof (student ID, Aadhar, or passport) to the venue during the events.
  6. In case of any disputes or clarifications, participants should contact the event coordinators. Final decisions of the organizers will be binding.
  7. Participants are expected to maintain ethical standards and sportsmanship throughout the event. Any violation may result in disqualification.
  8. The organizing committee reserves the right to modify event rules, schedule, or prizes with prior notification to all participants.
Important: By registering for ZENITH 2K26, you agree to abide by all rules and guidelines. Ensure you have read and understood the specific requirements for each event before registration. For any queries, reach out to us at zenith@kgist.edu.in or follow us on Instagram for the latest updates.
